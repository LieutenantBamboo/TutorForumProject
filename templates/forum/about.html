About page

Torum is a website created by Kyriacos Demetriou, Mathew Connor and Emily Youngs for WAD2 project. Torum is used to facilitate information between tutors and students who have questions on particular aspects of the course out with normal office hours. It's main purpose is for university's of Glasgow students to use Torum in order to ask any question they want about their subjects, and tutors can comment on their questions and answer.
